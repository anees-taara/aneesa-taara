    Balinese Grilled Chicken with a twist.



      Ingredients

      • 10 garlic cloves chopped
      • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh ginger
      • 3 fresh long red chiles, halved and seeded
      • 6 small shallots, halved
      • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
      • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
      • Salt and freshly ground pepper
      • butterflied chicken
      • 4 fresh bay leaves
      • 4 halves limes

        • Method


        In a food processor, combine the garlic, chiles, shallots, ginger and turmeric and pulse until finely chopped. Add the 1/4 cup of vegetable oil and pulse to a fine paste. Transfer the paste to a small skillet and cook over moderate heat, stirring until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Let the paste cool completely and season with salt and pepper.

        Set the chicken in a baking dish and rub the paste all over it. Place the bay leaves on top. Marinade overnight.

        Brushed lightly with oil. Transfer the chicken to the foil, skin side up. Cover and grill over moderate heat (350° to 375°) for 1 hour, until nearly cooked through. Add the limes to the foil and grill the chicken until the juices run clear when an inner thigh is pierced, about 15 minutes longer. Carve the chicken, transfer to a platter and serve with the limes.


        Air Fryer Crispy Chicken



          Ingredients

          • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
          • 1 teaspoon of salt
          • 1 cup buttermilk
          • 1 1/2 cups panko
          • 1 teaspoon of ground pepper
          • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
          • 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
          • 1 teaspoon salt
          • 1/2 teaspoon pepper, preferably white pepper
          • canola oil spray
          • Lime

            • Method


            Soak the chicken strips in the buttermilk with salt and ground pepper for 1 hour.

            Mix together the panko, garlic powder, white pepper, onion powder, sugar, and salt.

            Take a strip of chicken out of the buttermilk, shake gently, then coat in panko, pressing to ensure all sides are covered. Place in the fry basket of the air fryer, being careful not to crowd.

            Lightly spray the chicken with cooking oil spray. Cook at 400°F for 5 minutes, then flip and continue to cook for another five minutes, or until cooked through and crispy, up to 10 minutes more. Enjoy hot!
